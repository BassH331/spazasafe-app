## What is SpazaSafe?

A free app that helps you check if food products are real or fake before you buy.

**Scan. Snap. Know.**

## Why This Exists

In 2024, counterfeit food killed children and hospitalized hundreds in South Africa. Spaza shops serve millions daily, but there was no way to check if products are safe.

**Now there is.**
