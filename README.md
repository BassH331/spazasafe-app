In 2024, South Africa experienced a devastating food poisoning crisis. **22+ children lost their lives** and **890+ were hospitalized** due to counterfeit food products sold through informal retail channels.

Over **100,000 spaza shops** serve millions of South Africans daily, but until now, there was no way to verify product authenticity at the point of purchase.

**SpazaSafe changes that.**

## ğŸ§  The Technology

SpazaSafe uses **three layers of forensic analysis**:

| Analysis | Technology | What It Detects |
|----------|------------|-----------------|
| **Structure** | ResNet18 Deep Learning | Packaging design differences invisible to the human eye |
